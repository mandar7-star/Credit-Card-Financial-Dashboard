# 💳 Credit Card Financial  Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** focused on analyzing **credit card customer behavior and transaction insights**. It helps uncover patterns in revenue, customer segmentation, spending habits, and overall financial performance.

The project consists of two main dashboards:

* **Customer Dashboard**
* **Transaction Dashboard**


## 🧾 Dashboard 1: Credit Card Customer Report

This dashboard provides a detailed view of customer demographics and revenue distribution.

### 🔑 Key Metrics:

* **Total Revenue:** 55M
* **Total Interest Earned:** 8M
* **Total Income:** 576M
* **Customer Satisfaction Score (CSS):** 3.19


### 📌 Key Insights:

* Revenue is highest among **Businessmen and White-collar professionals**
* Customers aged **40–50 contribute the highest revenue**
* Majority revenue comes from **High and Medium income groups**
* Top performing states include:

  * TX, NY, CA, FL, NJ
* Married customers generate slightly higher revenue than single customers

### 📊 Visuals Included:

* Revenue by Age Group
* Revenue by Income Group
* Revenue by Education Level
* Revenue by Marital Status
* Revenue Trends (Weekly)
* Top States Analysis


## 💰 Dashboard 2: Credit Card Transaction Report

This dashboard focuses on transaction-level insights and spending behavior.

### 🔑 Key Metrics:

* **Total Revenue:** 55M
* **Total Transaction Amount:** 45M
* **Transaction Count:** 656K
* **Total Interest:** 8M
 

### 📌 Key Insights:

* Highest revenue generated in **Q3 (14.2M)**
* Most transactions happen via:

  * **Swipe (35M)**
  * Followed by Chip and Online
* Top expenditure categories:

  * Bills, Entertainment, Fuel, Grocery
* **Blue card category dominates revenue contribution**
* Customer acquisition cost is highest for **Blue cards (46M)**

### 📊 Visuals Included:

* Quarterly Revenue & Transaction Count
* Revenue by Expenditure Type
* Revenue by Customer Job
* Revenue by Education
* Card Category Analysis
* Customer Acquisition Cost
